Conversion Tips & Tricks

Master unit conversions with these helpful insights and shortcuts

Temperature Memory Tricks

Remember: Water freezes at 0°C (32°F) and boils at 100°C (212°F). Body temperature is 37°C (98.6°F).

Quick Formula: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Currency Rate Timing

Exchange rates update every 15 minutes during market hours. Best rates typically occur during London-New York overlap (1:30-6:00 PM IST).

Tip: Monitor rates for better business deals

Length Shortcuts

1 meter ≈ 3.3 feet, 1 inch = 2.54 cm exactly, 1 mile ≈ 1.6 km. Use these for quick mental calculations.

Memory: 1 yard = 3 feet = 36 inches

Weight Conversions

1 kg = 2.2 lbs (approximately). For cooking: 1 cup flour ≈ 120g, 1 cup sugar ≈ 200g, 1 cup butter ≈ 225g.

Cooking tip: 1 tablespoon = 15ml

Area Calculations

1 acre = 4,047 m² = 43,560 ft². For real estate: 1 square yard = 9 square feet. Always multiply length × width for area.

Real estate: 1 cent = 435.6 sq ft (South India)

Volume Essentials

1 liter = 1000 ml = 1.06 quarts. For cooking: 1 cup = 240ml, 1 gallon = 3.78 liters. Volume = length × width × height.

Fuel: 1 gallon ≈ 3.78 liters
