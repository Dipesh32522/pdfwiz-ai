Understanding GST Calculations

What is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. It replaced multiple cascading taxes and created a unified tax structure across the country.

GST Exclusive vs Inclusive

GST Exclusive

GST is added to the base amount. Formula: Total = Amount + (Amount × GST Rate)

GST Inclusive

GST is extracted from the total amount. Formula: GST = Total × GST Rate ÷ (100 + GST Rate)

Practical Examples

Product: ₹1,000 + 18% GST ₹1,180
Service: ₹5,000 + 18% GST ₹5,900
Luxury: ₹10,000 + 28% GST ₹12,800
